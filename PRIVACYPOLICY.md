# Privacy Policy  
**Slack Notifier**  
Effective Date: February 15, 2026  

## 1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes how **Slack Notifier** (“the App”), developed by **Arif Ansari**, collects, uses, and protects user information when you use the Google Workspace Add-on that connects Google Forms with Slack.

By using this application, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

## 2. Information We Access
Slack Notifier only accesses the minimum data required to perform its core functionality.

The App may access:

- Google Forms metadata and responses (only as needed to detect form submissions)
- Basic Google account information required for authentication (such as email address)
- Slack webhook or integration settings provided by the user

## 3. How Information Is Used
The data accessed by the App is used solely for the following purposes:

- Detecting Google Form submissions
- Sending notifications to the user’s configured Slack channel
- Providing and maintaining the core functionality of the add-on
- Troubleshooting and improving reliability

The App **does not** use data for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes.

## 4. Data Storage and Retention
- Slack Notifier does **not** permanently store Google Form responses or personal data.
- Data is processed temporarily only to deliver notifications to Slack.
- Configuration details (such as webhook URLs) may be stored securely only to enable continued operation of the integration.

## 5. Data Sharing
Slack Notifier does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

Data is only transmitted to:

- Slack, as required to send notifications initiated by the user’s configuration.

## 6. Security
Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect data during transmission and processing. Access to user data is restricted to functionality required for operation.

## 7. Third-Party Services
This App integrates with third-party services:

- Google Workspace (Google Forms)
- Slack

Use of these services is governed by their respective privacy policies.

## 8. Limited Use Disclosure
Slack Notifier’s use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Data is only used to provide or improve user-facing features that are visible and relevant to the user.

## 9. User Control
Users can:

- Disconnect the app at any time via their Google account permissions
- Remove Slack integrations whenever they choose
- Stop using the add-on at any time

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
